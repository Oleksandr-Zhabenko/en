The Apostle Paul writes that Old Testament events became prototypes and instruction (teaching) for his contemporary Christians, and better to understand for all Christians â€” because the last times continue from the time of the First Coming of Christ until the Second Coming of Christ.

Of course, one needs to pay attention to avoid such positions, because a person can be tempted similarly to them, even whilst being a Christian man or woman.
