Liturgy:
(Acts IV, 23-31)
Acts IV, 24 — πάντα τὰ ἐν αὐτοῖς - panta ta en autois - all that is in them. Where.

Acts IV, 27 — ἐν τῇ πόλει ταύτῃ - en te polei taute - in this city. That is, in Jerusalem. Where.

Acts IV, 30 — ‘ἐν τῷ’ - ‘en to’ - in that; with that. It is part of an unusual grammatical construction with cases. 'εἰς ἴασιν' - ‘eis iasin’ - ‘for healing; for curing’. The preposition eis indicates the purpose here.

Acts IV, 31 — ἐν ᾧ - en ho - in which. Where.
