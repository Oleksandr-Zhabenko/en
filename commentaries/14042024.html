(Hebrews VI, 13-20)
Hebrews VI, 17 — 'ἐμεσίτευσεν' - 'emesiteusen' - 'mediated; interposed; (did this) by mediation' - from 'μεσιτεύω' - 'to mediate; to interpose; to be between two different positions, people, etc.', i.e. God's oath is like a 'mediator' between God and people.

Hebrews VI, 20 — 'ἡμῶν' - 'emon' - 'us'.
