(Romans III, 19-26)
Romans III, 23 — 'ἥμαρτον' - 'emarton' - 'sinned' - from 'ἁμαρτάνω' - 'amartano'.

Romans III, 26 — 'δικαιοῦντα τὸν ἐκ πίστεως Ἰησοῦ' - 'dikaiounta ton ek pisteos iesou' - 'to justify those who are from ('of' (as the source of origin, the point of departure)) the faith of Jesus'. That is, if a person has been renewed through the faith in Christ, the faith of Christ, if their life has become an organic continuation of their faith in Christ, and faith in turn is the true beginning of their life, then the Lord is the One Who justifies them.
