(Philippians IV, 4-9)
Philippians IV, 9 — 'ἐμάθετε' - 'emathete' - 'learned; ascertained' - from 'μανθάνω' - 'manthano' - 'to learn; to ascertain'. 'ἐμοί' - 'emoi' - 'to me'.

For more information about the reading from the Apostle, please refer to the link:

(John XII, 1-18 )
John XII, 1 — 'ἡμερῶν' - 'emeron' - 'days'.

John XII, 6 — 'ἔμελεν' - 'emelen' - 'was concerned; cared' - from 'μέλει' - 'melei' - ‘to be concerned; to care’.

John XII, 7 — 'ἡμέραν' - 'emeran' - 'day'.

John XII, 8 — 'ἐμὲ' - 'eme' - 'Me'.

John XII, 16 — 'ἐμνήσθησαν' - 'emnesthesan' - 'recalled' - from 'μιμνήσκομαι' - 'mimneskomai' - 'to remember; to recall'.

John XII, 17 — 'ἐμαρτύρει' - 'emartyrei' - 'testified' - from 'μαρτυρέω' - 'martyreo' - 'to witness; to testify'.

For more information about the Gospel reading, please refer to the following links:
