(Luke X, 16-21)
Since the Archangel Michael directly led the struggle of the angels, who were faithful to God, against the proud and envious Lucifer and the other angels who obeyed him, the reading in which Jesus testifies to those events is also dedicated to the angels.

Luke X, 16 — 'εμου' - 'emou' - 'Me'. 'εμε' - 'eme' - 'Me' ((by) Me).

Luke X, 21 — 'εμπροσθεν' - 'emprosthen' - 'before; in front of the face'.

For more information on Luke X, 16-21, see here:
