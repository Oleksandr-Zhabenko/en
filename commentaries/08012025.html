(Mark VI, 7-13)
Mark VI, 8 — ‘εἰς ὁδὸν’ - ‘eis hodon’ - ‘in the way’. ‘εἰς τὴν ζώνην’ - ‘eis ten zonen’ - ‘in the belt; in the purse (purses were in the belts at that time)’.

Mark VI, 10 — ‘εἰς οἰκίαν’ - ‘eis oikian’ - ‘into the house’.

Mark VI, 11 — ‘εἰς μαρτύριον’ - ‘eis martyrion’ - ‘into testimony; for testimony’. The preposition ‘eis’ here indicates the purpose of the action.
