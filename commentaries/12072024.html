(Romans IX, 6-19)
Romans IX, verses 8-9 tell us what importance is attached to the promise. These words refer to God's children. God promised an heir to Abraham — and the child born of this promise, through this promise, as its fulfilment from God, is God's because he is really from Him. And of course, the God's is higher than the human's, and therefore higher than the corporeal.
