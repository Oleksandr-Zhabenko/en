(Matthew XXII, 15 — XXIII, 39)
Matthew XXII, 17, 25 — 'ἡμῖν' - 'emin' - 'to us'.

Matthew XXII, 23 — 'ἡμέρᾳ' - 'emera' - 'day'.

Matthew XXII, 32 — 'εἰμι' - 'eimi' - (I) 'am'. It is used for emphasis.

Matthew XXII, 46 — 'ἡμέρας' - 'emeras' - 'of the day'.

Matthew XXIII, 13 — 'ἔμπροσθεν' - 'emprosthen' - 'before; in front of the face'.

Matthew XXIII, 30 — 'ἤμεθα' - 'emetha' - 'lived'. 'ἡμέραις' - 'emerais' - 'days'. 'ἡμῶν' - 'emon' - 'us' (our).
