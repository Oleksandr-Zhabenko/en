Dedicated to Emma Kok.

(3 John I, 1-15)
3 John I, 4 — 'ἐμὰ' - 'ema' - 'my'.

3 John I, 6 — 'ἐμαρτύρησάν' - 'emartyresan' - 'testified' - from 'μαρτυρέω'.

3 John I, 8, 12 — 'ἡμεῖς' - 'emeis' - 'we'.

3 John I, 9, 10 — 'ἡμᾶς' - 'emas' - 'us'.

3 John I, 12 — 'ἡμῶν' - 'emon' - 'us' (our).

For the reading from the Apostle, please refer to the link:
https://oleksandr-zhabenko.github.io/en/commentaries/20022023.html

(Luke XIX, 29-40; XXII, 7-39)
Luke XXII, 7 — 'ἡμέρα' - 'emera' - 'day'.

Luke XXII, 8 — 'ἡμῖν' - 'emin' - 'to us'.

Luke XXII, 19 — 'ἐμὴν' - 'emen' - 'My' (Me; in the original, literally 'in My remembrance', for 'remembrance' is feminine).

Luke XXII, 21, 37 — 'ἐμοῦ' - 'emou' - 'Me'.

Luke XXII, 27 — 'εἰμι' - 'eimi' - (I) 'am'. It is used for emphasis.

Luke XXII, 37 — 'ἐμοί' - 'emoi' - 'to Me'.

For the Gospel readings, please refer to the link:
