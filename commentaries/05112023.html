Dedicated to Emma Kokк.

(Galatians VI, 11-18)

Galatians VI, 11 — ἐμῇ — 'eme' — 'my' (mine).

Galatians VI, 14, 18 — 'ἐμοὶ' — 'emoi' — (to) 'me' (for me). 'ἡμῶν' — 'emon' — 'us' (our).

It is a continuation of the reading, which is referred to by the link:

https://oleksandr-zhabenko.github.io/en/commentaries/20092023.html

I wrote about this reading earlier, see here:

I would also add that verse 15 refers to the new creation as the renewal of the human being through faith in Christ, i.e. how a person is actually changing through Christ in the Holy Spirit. This change is much more important than the ceremonial component of the Law of Moses.
