Paul goes on to show that the Law did not contain the fullness of faith, and thus we cannot assume that we should limit ourselves to it, or that there is and will be anything better than the Law. To this end, he compares quotations from various passages of the Old Testament that show in a figurative way that the faith fulfilled in Christ will replace the ceremonialism of the Law, being superior and being the fulfilment of what was promised through and before the Law.
