From the XI chapter – about patience and difficulties, suffering and more physical work, in the XII chapter – about the spiritual and the revelation of God.

It can also be noticed that Paul does not encourage anyone to boast in such a way, but speaks about himself from the third person, from the side, also it was not possible to convey the word of God to those people in any other way.
