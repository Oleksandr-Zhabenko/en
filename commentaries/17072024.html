(Romanians XI, 2-12)
Romans XI, 6 — ‘ἐξ ἔργων’ - ‘ex ergon’ - ‘from (by) works’.

‘The chosen have received’ — that is, the chosen have become righteous. See here:
https://oleksandr-zhabenko.github.io/en/commentaries/16072024.html

Romans XI, 8 — ‘ἡμέρας’ - ‘emeras’ - ‘day’. Here again Paul, following the Old Testament people, does not distinguish between God's permission and will, as he quotes the Old Testament where this is evident. God allowed them to be in unbelief, not willed it.
