(Zechariah VIII, 7-17)
Zechariah VIII, 8 — 'beemet' - in truth - from 'emeth' - certainty, verity, truth, fidelity, trustworthiness - in the Hebrew original feminine.

Zechariah VIII, 16 — 'emet' - 'the truth, the verity' - from 'emeth' - 'certainty, truth, verity, fidelity, worthiness of trust' - in the Hebrew original feminine.

(Zechariah VIII, 19-23)
Zechariah VIII, 19 — 'wehaemet' - therefore, the truth - from 'emeth' - certainty, truth, verity, fidelity, worthiness of trust - in the Hebrew original feminine.

Zechariah VIII, 23 — 'immakem' - 'with you' - from 'im' - 'with'.

Here we can also notice a very deep thought that human beings are intended by God as truth, as peace, as trustworthiness, as certainty and faith. After all, the commandments say: You shall love the Lord… you shall love man... You shall love truth and peace.... And so, God intended that human beings should be the truth and peace, because it pleased God to reveal His truth to people and in human beings — first and foremost in Jesus Christ, our Lord and Saviour, Who is the Original Image for every human being, created in the image of God.
