(Mark VIII, 1-10)
Mark VIII, 1 — ‘Ἐν ἐκείναις ταῖς ἡμέραις’ - ‘En ekeinais tais hemerais’ - ‘In those days’. A common expression.

Mark VIII, 3 — ‘εἰς οἶκον’ - ‘eis oikon’ - ‘into the house’. It is singular instead of the expected plural. ‘ἐν τῇ ὁδῷ’ - ‘en te hodo’ - ‘on the way’. ‘ἀπὸ μακρόθεν’ - ‘apo makrothen’ - ‘from afar’. A common expression that also emphasises that they have left their places.

Mark VIII, 10 — ‘εἰς τὸ πλοῖον’ - ‘eis to ploion’ - ‘into the boat’. ‘εἰς τὰ μέρη Δαλμανουθά’ - ‘eis ta mere Dalmanoutha’ - ‘in the district of Dalmanutha’.
