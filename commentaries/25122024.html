(Galatians IV, 4-7)
Galatians IV, 4, 5 — ‘ἐκ γυναικός’ - ‘ek gynaikos’ - ‘out of a woman'. ‘ὑπὸ νόμον’ - ‘ypo nomon’ - ‘under the Law’. The preposition ‘ypo’ is here with the accusative case.

Galatians IV, 6 — ‘εἰς τὰς καρδίας’ - ‘eis tas kardias’ - ‘into the hearts’.
