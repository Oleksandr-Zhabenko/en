(Romans XIII, 11 – XIV, 4)

Today’s daily reading is a call to wake up from spiritual sleep, to throw away evil deeds, thoughts, words and intentions, instead to be such ones that when the Lord will come, when the secret will be revealed, we will be in the light and not in the darkness.

Put on the Lord Jesus Christ – perhaps a person is not ready to completely renew his / her life, to completely change in one moment, to fill his / her whole heart, soul and mind with light, to approach perfection, but every person can change in Christ, to what, in fact, Paul urges. A person can dress himself / herself – similarly he / she can take good thoughts, he / she can fulfill the commandment, he / she can serve Christ and his neighbors, etc.

Do not turn care for the body into lust – literally do not create thoughts about the body in advance (as a destination) in lust – very deep words, people can create (something) in (to, into) lust, that is, they can creatively form their sins , and it is precisely this latter that must be guarded against and avoided.

Accept the weak in faith not for disputes about views — not in (as a destination) judging (distinguishing) thoughts, plans, movements of the heart — do not burden the unconfirmed with great vigilance, on the contrary, you need to show mutual support, unity and solidarity in good, in Christ.

The desire to correct one’s neighbour may come not from love for God, for him / her, and for oneself, but from other motives, and then such a desire can even lead to distrust in God that He cares for every person. This is what Paul teaches to avoid.

(Matthew VI, 14-21)

About the importance of forgiveness – here you use the word let go, send away from yourself – that is, not hold. And even then God will not hold. It talks about the importance, but does not explicitly say how it should be done (the way). But the first thing you can do is look at it with God’s eyes, that is accept God in this situation.
