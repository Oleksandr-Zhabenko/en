Acts XXI, 26, 28, 29 — ‘εἰς τὸ ἱερόν’ - ‘eis to hieron’ - ‘to the temple’. Where to.

Acts XXI, 27 — ‘οἱ ἀπὸ τῆς Ἀσίας Ἰουδαῖοι’ - ‘hoi apo tes Asias Ioudaioi’ - ‘those Jews from Asia’. The preposition ‘apo’ here indicates that they completely left Asia and migrated. ‘ἐν τῷ ἱερῷ’ – ‘en to hiero’ – ‘in the temple’. Where.
