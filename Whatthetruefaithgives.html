What the true faith gives.

While discussing the people's expectations of the true faith I considered the following.

The true faith gives opportunities (possibilities, abilities) to live in the truth. It allows you to interact with, to touch, to be in the areas of being where the false faith doesn't allow to. To be laconic, it gives you the possibilities (abilities, opportunities). All and every ones that exist.

It protects and prevents you from the illusive, pseudo possibilities (abilities, opportunities).

As to the Greek-Orthodox faith, it allows you to associate with True God, the Holy Trinity. It gives you the abilities, possibilities and opportunities to overcome even the most insignificant sin. Not just associate, but, moreover, to be in the communion and unity, to live in Him. It gives you the strengths and willingness to be invincible in the struggle and leads to the victory. It allows you to build the peaceful world and to find your own place in it. It allows you to develop your talents and to express yourself. Well, to be laconic, I won't continue to count more and more.
